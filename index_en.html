Pinjetta Collections offers high-quality ethically produced t-shirts and hoodies with prints designed by Pinja. All the garments are made out of organic cotton and the designs are printed with environmentally friendly low-waste inks. Pinjetta Collections is powered by Teemill and Rapanui.

Pinjetta Boutique is an experimental shop of unique clothes. In there you'll find carefully selected second hand clothes having a new face with prints designed and handprinted by Pinja.
